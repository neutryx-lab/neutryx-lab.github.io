Neutryx‑Core (Open‑Source)

High‑performance JAX‑based numerical library for derivative pricing, risk, and XVA, featuring automatic differentiation, PDE solvers, and clean extensibility. Interactive demo pricer available on the page. Released under a permissive open‑source license.

Neutryx‑AI (Under IP Review)

A constraint-aware neural universal approximator for model-free numerical reasoning. Implements a general adaptive constrained optimisation framework with meta-gradient control to ensure mathematical, physical, and probabilistic consistency. Provides a domain-agnostic architecture applicable to calibration, inverse problems, and safe output regulation. Subject of UCLB invention disclosure.
