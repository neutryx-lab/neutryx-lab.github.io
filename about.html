About Neutryx

Neutryx Lab operates at the intersection of machine learning and computational science. Our long-term goal is to advance the accuracy, transparency, and efficiency of numerical simulation and risk modeling through data-driven approaches.

The initiative originated within University College London and collaborates with academic and industrial partners. Current activities focus on research dissemination, collaboration, and open-science outreach.
